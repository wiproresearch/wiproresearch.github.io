About

Research that builds a better world.

Established in 2024, Wipro Research represents our commitment to maintaining technological leadership in the industrial engineering sector.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses cross-functional teams working on breakthrough technologies that shape the future of manufacturing, automation, and infrastructure solutions — translating ambitious research into industrial reality.

We are part of Wipro Infrastructure Engineering, a diverse industrial engineering business with expertise spanning over Hydraulics, Aerospace, Water treatment, Additive manufacturing and Automation solutions with 33 state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities across 4 continents.

Research areas

Five domains. One mission.

Our research portfolio spans the full stack of modern industrial engineering — from the physics of motion and materials to the intelligence layered above them. Each domain is led by dedicated experts and supported by deep partnerships with academia and industry.

01 / Domain

Motion Components & Autonomous Control

Advancing industrial automation through innovative control systems. Our research spans precision movement in electro-hydraulic equipment, autonomous navigation, and AI-driven predictive maintenance.

02 / Domain

Electronics, Network & Communication

Developing robust industrial IoT solutions and next-generation communication systems. We focus on seamless connectivity for smart manufacturing environments and infrastructure networks.

03 / Domain

Computer Vision & Software 2.0

Pushing the boundaries of computer vision and AI applications in industrial settings — from automated quality control to intelligent layout design, making factories smarter and more efficient.

04 / Domain

Material Science

Innovating with advanced materials for improved performance and sustainability. Our research covers composite materials, smart materials, and eco-friendly alternatives for industrial applications.

05 / Domain

Manufacturing Process

Revolutionizing manufacturing through advanced processes and technologies. We explore cutting-edge welding techniques, non-destructive testing methods, and additive manufacturing solutions.
