Academic Partnerships

Research, in good company.

Wipro Research is committed to fostering strong academic partnerships that drive innovation in industrial engineering. We believe in creating meaningful collaborations with leading universities and research institutions to advance technology and nurture the next generation of researchers.

Our approach

How we work with academia.

Our model is built around openness, mutual benefit, and the discipline of translating fundamental research into real-world industrial outcomes. Every collaboration is designed around the institution's strengths and goals.

  • 01
    Open Collaboration Model We welcome partnerships with universities and research institutions globally — across disciplines, geographies, and stages of research.
  • 02
    Industry-Academia Synergy A focus on translating academic research into practical industrial solutions, with clear paths from paper to prototype to deployment.
  • 03
    Multi-disciplinary Research Support for cross-functional research spanning all engineering domains — because the hardest problems rarely sit inside a single department.
  • 04
    Research Infrastructure Access to state-of-the-art industrial facilities and real-world implementation opportunities your campus simply cannot replicate.

Partnership benefits

What partner institutions can expect.

Industry expertise & mentorship

Direct guidance from Wipro engineers and researchers actively shipping industrial systems at scale.

Real-world challenges

Access to live industrial problems with tangible engineering constraints — the kind of work that defines a career.

Research funding support

Sponsored research arrangements, equipment access, and student stipends for selected programs.

Technology commercialisation

A defined path from research output to deployed product — with the supply chain and partners to make it happen.

Joint IP development

Transparent, structured agreements for jointly developed intellectual property — fair to both sides from day one.

Publications & patents

Encouragement and support for publication, conference participation and patent filings — academic credit where it's earned.

Ways to collaborate.

Our collaborative framework is flexible and can be customised to align with your institution's research goals and strengths. The most common starting points:

Doctoral research programs

Industry-integrated PhDs co-supervised with your faculty.

Joint research projects

Technology development initiatives

Targeted programs translating research into deployable systems.

Innovation programs

Hackathons, fellowships, and visiting researcher schemes.
